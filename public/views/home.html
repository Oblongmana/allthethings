All the things

This is the place for your all your things! With All The Things, you can keep an inventory of everything you own, or just those things that you care most about.

Key features include:

  • Location Designation
  • Custom Tracking
  • Custom Container Types
  • Storage Options
  • Dynamic Grid Operations
Tracking
Anything can be designated as a location. You can easily look up which items you have in storage, what room they are in, or even what box they are in. You can also designate Other Common Locations of items, so you know what locations to check when things go misplaced.
Be able to get your things back! All The Things allows you to have an itemized list of everything you own. If the worst happens and you lose your things in a disaster, all the things will be able to supply insurance companies with the information they need to get your things back.

Item information includes:

  • Price of the item at purchase
  • Replacement prices
  • When the item was purchased
  • A sentamental rating
  • Warrenty periods
  • ...And anything else you can think of!
